Privacy Policy for dWeeMart


Effective Date: 20th September, 2023
Introduction

Welcome to dWeeMart! This Privacy Policy is designed to help you understand how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your personal information. By using our mobile application and services, you consent to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

1. User-Provided Information: When you sign up for dWeeMart, we may collect information such as your name, email address, phone number, and location. You may also provide additional information, such as a profile picture or other personal details.
2. Listing Information: To create and manage listings, you may provide information about the items you want to sell or buy, including photos, descriptions, and prices.
3. Transaction Information: We collect information related to your transactions on dWeeMart, such as the items you buy or sell, payment details, and shipping information.
4. Device Information: We automatically collect information about the devices you use to access dWeeMart, including device type, operating system, and unique device identifiers.
5. Log Data: Our servers may automatically log information, including your IP address, browser type, and pages viewed, to analyze usage patterns and improve our services.
How We Use Your Information

1. Personalization: We use your information to provide a personalized experience on dWeeMart, including showing relevant listings and recommendations.
2. Communication: We may use your contact information to send you notifications, updates, and marketing communications related to our services. You can opt out of marketing communications at any time.
3. Transaction Fulfillment: Your information is used to facilitate transactions between buyers and sellers, including processing payments and arranging item pickup or delivery.
4. Analytics: We analyze data to improve our services, enhance security, and develop new features.
Sharing Your Information

We may share your information with:

- Other users as necessary to facilitate transactions.
- Third-party service providers to help us operate the app (e.g., payment processors, analytics tools).

Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your information but cannot guarantee complete security. You are responsible for maintaining the security of your account.

Your Choices
You can access and update your account information in the app settings. You can also request to delete your account by contacting us.
